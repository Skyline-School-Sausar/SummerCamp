🌟 Our Mission

Technoventor Nagpur is a tech-education team passionate about bringing coding, web design, robotics, and digital skills to school students across Maharashtra. We believe every child deserves to be a creator — not just a consumer — of technology.

🏆 What We Do

We design and run hands-on technology camps, workshops, and after-school programs. From building websites to programming robots, our curriculum is made to be exciting, age-appropriate, and immediately practical.

🗾 Why Manga & Fun?

We believe learning is best when it's joyful. Just like Naruto never gave up on becoming Hokage, our students never give up on cracking code! We mix storytelling, games, and real projects to make tech unforgettable.

📍 Based In

Nagpur, Maharashtra. We partner with schools across the Vidarbha region and beyond, running summer camps, science fairs, and STEM workshops for students from Grade 1 to Grade 12.
